Mia Stories Together

Interactive engine for visual novels with a custom-designed UI. On the platform mia.promotich.team, authors can create their own stories using a Node-based system — and this app was built specifically to bring those stories to life. The game features a wide variety of choices, branching storylines, character stats, and relationship mechanics. Implemented systems include monetization, user authentication, in-app purchases (IAP), ads integration, Cloud Content Delivery, and more. Most impressively, the entire application was designed and developed solely by me.
